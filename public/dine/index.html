Cocina 214 would love to cater your next event! Let us cater your Office Lunches or Corporate Functions, Engagement Parties, Bridal Showers, Rehearsal Dinners, Baby Showers, Alumni Gatherings, Birthdays, Anniversaries, Holiday Parties and much more!

We can bring our award-winning Tex-Mex/Mexican to your work, residence or other location with advanced notice.
